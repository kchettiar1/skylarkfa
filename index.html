Specialist Farm Advice

Why work with me?

Tailor-made service

From a one-off site visit for a privately owned pond to a full management plan with annual monitoring on a large estate, we offer a complete and bespoke service based on your needs.

We are land managers

We not only have experience of working with a range of land owners but also managing and surveying our own nature reserves. This all adds up to years of hands on conservation experience and a service that you can trust

Proven competency

Our team of ecologists and farm advisors are members of the Chartered Institute of Ecology and Environmental Management (CIEEM) and Arcadian is a member of the Association of Wildlife Trust Consultancies (AWTC).

Supporting conservation

All profits from our consulting work are donated back to the charity to support conservation in Hampshire and the Isle of Wight.

Services

Through Skylark Farm Advice we provide a range of services including countryside stewardship; habitat and species surveys, and advice; compliance and greening guidance; soil management; meeting regulatory requirements, workshops and training and assurance schemes.

With over 20 years combined experience of providing farm advice in Hampshire and the surrounding areas, our advisors specialise in working with farmers to maximise the environmental potential of their land while supporting the farm business.

Amanda Ingham, Senior Farm Advisor, focuses on delivery of resource protection advice in partnership with Affinity Water, South East Water and Natural England. She has a BSc in Ecology and is a member of the Chartered Institute of Ecology and Environmental Management. Alison and Amanda both hold certificates in BASIS Soil and Water Management and Conservation Management. Both advisors enjoy working with farmers to integrate conservation measures within a successful farming business. Each advisor has over 10 years experience of working on agri-environment schemes including Countryside Stewardship.
